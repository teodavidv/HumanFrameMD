Back Care Designed For Real Life

SpineRelief was created with one clear purpose: to help people break free from chronic back and nerve pain without relying on medication, invasive treatments, or constant appointments.

We know how exhausting back pain can be. When movement hurts and rest doesn’t restore you, everyday life becomes harder than it should be. That frustration is what pushed us to build a better solution.

From the beginning, we’ve worked with spine specialists, physical therapists, and pain-management professionals to design products that support the body’s natural recovery process, safely and effectively.

Here to Help You Feel Better Every Day

Back pain affects how you move, sleep, and show up each day. Our approach focuses on relieving pressure, easing stiffness, and supporting better movement, all from the comfort of home.

Our goal is simple: help you regain comfort, mobility, and confidence without complicated routines or ongoing therapy visits.

What Guides Everything We Do

Every solution needs a clear path. At SpineRelief, our mission is simple, and our values guide every decision we make.

We listen closely and with curiosity, to understand your needs and make informed decisions together.

We eagerly embrace challenges, relentlessly research, and approach roadblocks with grit and good will.

We are dedicated to expanding access and delivering care to a diverse population, supported by a diverse team.

We acknowledge that we don’t know what we don’t know. We admit when we’re wrong, to our patients and to one another, and strive to do better.

We take a scrappy, experimental approach to innovation. If we can't find what our patients and clinicians need to thrive, we build it.
